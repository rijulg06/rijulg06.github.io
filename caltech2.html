Overview

I am continuing research at Caltech (see my summer project here!) in Professor Lulu Qian's Lab. We are now looking at unsupervised learning neural networks in DNA systems, upgrading the capabilities of such classifying networks.

This new system requires a weight normalization step. My partner and I are responsible for designing a motif to achieve this task.

We will be drawing on our knowledge from our summer project, as well as other motifs that could help us normalize the network weights.
